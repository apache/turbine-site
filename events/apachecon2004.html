ApacheCon 2004 Talks

These talks might be of special interest for Turbine developers:

  • Kishore Senji - TU24: Revving up Turbine
  • Henning P. Schmiedehausen - MO12: Logging and Configuration - Demystifying the banes of App development. Turbine uses both, commons-configuration and commons-logging covered in this talk.
  • Henning P. Schmiedehausen - WE01: Jakarta Velocity - An Overview. Turbine uses Velocity as its primary View layer.
